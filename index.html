Our Journey

1971 - The Beginning

Starbucks opened its first store in Seattle’s Pike Place Market, focusing on high-quality roasted coffee beans. This humble shop set the foundation for a global coffee phenomenon, redefining how the world enjoys its favorite brew.

1987 - Expansion

Howard Schultz acquired Starbucks and transformed it into an espresso-driven brand. Under his leadership, the company embraced innovation, setting the stage for expansion that introduced the iconic coffee experience beyond Seattle.

2000s - Going Global

Starbucks reached global markets, opening stores worldwide. From Tokyo to Paris, the brand became synonymous with premium coffee and a lifestyle, bringing people together with every cup served.

Present Day

Today, Starbucks continues to innovate and connect communities, offering sustainable practices and modern experiences while staying true to its mission of inspiring and nurturing the human spirit.
